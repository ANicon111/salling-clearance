How to Use Salling Deal Hunter

Welcome to the Deal Hunter! Follow these quick steps to set up your search and find the best promotions.

1. Add Your Target Stores

In the Target Stores box, let the app know where you want to shop. You must enter one store per line.

  • For a specific local store: Type the brand, a colon, and the location.
    Example: NETTO: Lufthavnen or FOTEX: Aarhus
  • For general leaflet deals: Just type the brand name.
    Example: BILKA

2. Enter Product Keywords

In the Product Keywords box, type what you are looking for. You can search for multiple items at once by separating them with a comma or space.

Example: brød, mælk

3. Search & Save

  • Click Search to fetch your deals. The app will automatically save your store list and keywords for the next time you visit.
  • Click the Copy Icon next to the search button if you want to copy your current setup to your clipboard to share or save elsewhere.
System Note on Data Refreshing:
To keep the application fast and avoid putting excessive strain on the servers, every search request is cached for 5 seconds. Additionally, the main store and leaflet data are cached for one hour. If you don't see brand-new leaflet updates the exact second they drop, this caching system is why!
